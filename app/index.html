Buon giorno

I’m a twenty-two year old web designer, crafting and building beautiful websites for the award-winning Fueled London team.

In my spare time, I have a love-hate relationship with Tottenham Hotspur FC, indulge my imagination with LEGO bricks, and doodle glyphs for any number of potential typefaces.
