Buon giorno

I’m a twenty-two year old web designer, designing and building beautiful websites for the award-winning Fueled London team.

In my spare time, I have a season ticket for my love-hate relationship with THFC, indulge my imagination with LEGO bricks, and doodling glyphs for any number of potential typefaces.

For more of an insight into my time studying at the world-renown Department of Typography & Graphic Communication, at the University of Reading, or to find out more about my past employment give my CV a read.
